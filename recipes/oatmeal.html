Oatmeal

Description

A quick and easy oatmeal recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of quick oats
  • 2 cup of milk of choice
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • frozen blueberries
  • sugar free syrup

Steps

  1. Pour milk and oats into a bowl and microwave for 2 minutes
  2. Add protein powder to the bowl and mix
  3. Top with frozen blueberries and syrup
